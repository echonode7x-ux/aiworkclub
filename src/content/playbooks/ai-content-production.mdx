# AI Content Production Engine

Creating content consistently is the hardest part of inbound marketing. This playbook shows you how to leverage AI to turn a single idea into a month's worth of multi-channel content.

## Phase 1: Ideation with Perplexity

Instead of guessing what your audience wants, use Perplexity to analyze current trends.

**Prompt:**
> "Analyze the current trending topics in [Your Niche] for 2024. Provide 5 unique angles that haven't been over-discussed."

## Phase 2: Long-form Drafting with Claude

Take your chosen angle and use Claude 3.5 Sonnet to draft a comprehensive cornerstone piece (like a newsletter or blog post).

**Prompt:**
> "Act as a world-class copywriter. Write a 1,500-word newsletter about [Topic]. Use an engaging, conversational tone. Include practical examples, a clear thesis, and actionable takeaways."

## Phase 3: Repurposing with ChatGPT

Feed the long-form content into ChatGPT and ask it to slice it into different formats.

- 3 LinkedIn Hooks + Posts
- 1 Twitter/X Thread (7-10 tweets)
- 1 Short-form video script (TikTok/Reels)

## Phase 4: Automation

Use Make.com or Zapier to automatically schedule these outputs into your social media management tool (like Buffer or Hypefury).
