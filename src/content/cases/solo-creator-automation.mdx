# The One-Person Media Empire

The creator economy is booming, but burnout is the number one reason creators quit. This case study breaks down how a solo creator running a popular tech newsletter automates 70% of their business.

## The Workflow Stack

1. **Content Curation (Feedly + AI)**
   The creator reads 50+ blogs. Instead of reading them manually, an AI agent summarizes the top 10 most relevant articles daily and drops them into a Notion database.

2. **Drafting (Claude 3.5 Sonnet)**
   The creator writes the bullet points and original thoughts. Claude 3.5 Sonnet expands these into full paragraphs while matching the creator's unique tone of voice.

3. **Sponsorship Management (Make.com)**
   When a sponsor books a slot via a Typeform:
   - Make.com automatically creates a Stripe invoice.
   - A Google Drive folder is created for their assets.
   - An automated email is sent to the sponsor requesting their copy and logo.

## Result

The creator saves over 20 hours a week on administrative tasks and first-draft writing, allowing them to focus entirely on strategy, networking, and deep-dive research.
