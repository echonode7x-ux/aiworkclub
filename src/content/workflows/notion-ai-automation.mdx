# Automate Your Life with Notion AI

Notion AI isn't just a writing assistant; it can fundamentally change how you organize data. Here is a step-by-step workflow to automate your daily summaries and meeting notes.

## 1. Automated Meeting Summaries

Instead of manually typing out next steps after a meeting:
1. Paste your raw meeting transcript into a Notion page.
2. Add a **Custom AI Block**.
3. Use the prompt: `Extract all action items, assignees, and deadlines from this transcript. Format as a markdown checklist.`

## 2. Database Autofill

If you have a CRM or a Reading List in Notion, you can use AI to automatically fill in properties.
- **Example**: Create a text column called "Summary". Set it as an AI Autofill column.
- Provide instructions: `Read the content of this page and write a 2-sentence TL;DR summary.`
- Notion will automatically run this prompt every time you add a new page.

## 3. Weekly Review Generation

Create a template for your Weekly Review.
Add an AI block that reads your daily journal entries for the week and answers:
- What were the biggest wins?
- What took up the most time?
- What should be improved next week?
